Nicaraguense Arroz A La Valenciana

Arroz A La Valenciana is a delicious Nicaraguan version of Valencian paella. It features shredded chicken, sausage, carrots, celery and bell peppers. You can enjoy this as a main entrée or as a side dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked white rice
  • 1 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 2 chopped classic hotdog sausages
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp salted butter
  • 1/2 cup chopped white onions
  • 1/3 cup chopped carrots
  • 1/3 cup chopped celery
  • 1/3 cup green peppers
  • 1 diced tomato
  • 1 tbsp chopped garlic
  • 1/2 cup chicken stock
  • 1–2 tbsp adobo seasoning
  • 1/2 packet sazon with achiote
  • 1 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • Sweet peas (optional)
  • Cilantro garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook rice with chicken stock until tender and refrigerate overnight.
  2. Boil chicken with garlic and adobo until fully cooked.
  3. Shred chicken and reserve broth.
  4. Sauté onions, celery, carrots, peppers and tomatoes.
  5. Add shredded chicken and broth; simmer 5 minutes.
  6. Add rice and mix equal parts chicken and rice.
  7. Fold in chopped hotdogs and cook 2 minutes.
  8. Add sazon, ketchup and Worcestershire sauce.
  9. Add peas if desired.
  10. Serve with cilantro and enjoy.
